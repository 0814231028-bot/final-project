Fun Facts:
Hamsters are nocturnal. Nocturnal means they are most active at night.
They are solitary animals, beware of putting two hamsters together.
Roborovski or Robo hamsters, are the hardest to tame!
Some hamsters can run six miles every night, which is why a wheel is a must have.


Remember to cherish every moment with your hamster, they only have three years.
They may be a chapter in yours, but you're their whole book.
