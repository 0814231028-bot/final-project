Bond and Care

How to Care:

  • Use enough bedding that enourages burrowing, 8 feet deep is a great amount
  • Fill out dust baths, NO WATER, hamsters do not belong in water
  • Scatter food around their encolsure, hamsters are scavengers
  • Wood they can run on can trim their nails
  • Cardboard is a good chew toy
  • Running wheels are a MUST HAVE!!!

How to Bond:

  1. Allow your hamster to explore and get comfortable in their new environment
  2. Don't hide your presence, let them get used to seeing you
  3. Offer paper or cardboard for their nests and burrows
  4. Slowly start rubbing your scent on your offerings, let them get used to your smell
  5. Start placing the offerings on your hand, let them climb on
  6. Offer treats and lower your hand to look non threatening
